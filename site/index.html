HOW TO ENTER COURSES ON MYED

PROCESS FOR 2024-2025

  • Ensure you have your MyEd password ready to go. If you are having trouble logging in to your MyEdBC account please email: elginpark@surreyschools.ca. Type in the Subject Line: Portal Login. In the body of the email please include student's full name, grade, and which account needs to be reset (Student or Parent).
  • Explore this website so that you are familiar with all courses that Elgin Park offers. Please watch the videos and pay attention to flow charts and explanations to ensure you are choosing the course that is right for you.
  • If applying to a specialized course or program, please fill out the appropriate application form (found under FORMS) and return to your alpha counsellor.
  • Log into MyEd to enter your courses.
