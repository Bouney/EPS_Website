Fine Arts

Media Arts (Film & Photography)

Media Arts (Film) & Photography courses provide students with a guided process that improves photography, film making, digital art and problem-based learning skills. General requirements: research, concept development, draft development, final copy-editing, and self-critiquing. Opportunities for self-exploration, experimentation, contract projects, and exhibiting pieces. Student success depends on personal maturity and willingness to do "prescribed" learning tasks methodically and timely.

Projects: To facilitate "real" learning, students are provided with clear steps and expectations for their projects (four in number), with reasonable timelines (typically four weeks). Assignment expectations are reviewed many times. Outside-of-class time is available for clarification. Weekly skill-building tutorials and in-class assignments are also completed.

Media Arts 9/10 (Film Focus)

9 10 Elective
