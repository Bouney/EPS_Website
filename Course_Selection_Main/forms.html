Grade 7 course selection guide

Accessible version
  1. All students must take:
    • English 8
    • Social Studies 8
    • Mathematics 8
    • Science 8
    • Physical Education 8
    • A French or Spanish option depending on Grade 7
    • An ADST rotation
  2. Decide if Band is right for you.
    • If yes: Choose Band + PE and an ADST rotation
    • If no: Choose PE, a Fine Arts rotation and an ADST rotation
  3. Fill out your Grade 8 course selection form.
    • This is provided to parents of Grade 7 students entering Elgin Park.
  4. Contact your alpha counsellor if you have any questions!
  5. Days to remember:
    • Course selection due: February 7
    • Whale of a Day (Grade 7s visit Elgin Park): April 23
